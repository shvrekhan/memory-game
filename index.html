Memory Game

Instructions

  1. The game will display a set of cards face down on the screen.
  2. Click on two cards to flip them over and reveal their images.
  3. If the images match, they will stay face up and you can continue to the next pair.
  4. If the images do not match, the cards will flip back over and you can try again.
  5. The game ends when all pairs have been found.
